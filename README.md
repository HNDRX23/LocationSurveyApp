# Background
This app was created out of a family emergency situation in Guayaquil, Ecuador — a city with serious safety concerns. When my stepmom's son left the household unexpectedly, we needed a way to locate him quickly to ensure his safety.
With limited options and urgent circumstances, I built a simple app disguised as a survey that, when opened, would capture and report the device's location. The goal was solely to find him and bring him home safely.
Purpose

## Locate a missing family member in a high-risk urban environment
Enable family to respond quickly and ensure his safety
Built as a last resort when other contact methods failed

## Context
Guayaquil has significant security challenges, and a young person alone on the streets represented a genuine safety risk. This tool was used exclusively within the family and only for the purpose of locating him during this emergency.
Note
This tool was built for a one-time family emergency. Location data was not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.
